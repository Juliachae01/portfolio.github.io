UX-project: Caspeco "Mitt Schema"

Internship 2021


Caspeco is a fast-growing company, located in Uppsala that offers operating systems including salary, booking, schedule, cash, payment, analysis for the restaurant industry. Today, they deliver cloud-based digital solutions, both in desktop and mobile applications for restaurateurs and their employees.

To evaluate and redesign Caspeco's "Mitt Schema" product

During my internship period, I worked mainly with evaluating and redesigning Caspeco's "Mitt schema" (My schedule in English) product.
There are two different types of schedules:
The first is "Stora schema" which can create and add work shifts for staff, it is used by the restaurant managers and administrators who have the power to manage staff.
The second is called "Mitt schema", where all regular staff can check their work schedule, manage their available working hours and apply for absence.
It is used by about 12,000 users while the Stora schema is used by 1,000 people.
The problem, however, is that "My Schedule" has not been developed for so many years because Caspeco's close customers are restaurant managers who do not use the mobile app, so they focus more on the Big Schedule.
Therefore, my task was to explore user problems, pinpoint pain points and sketch and design solutions.

Design thinking iteration process

I iterated the Design thinking process around 4 times. I empathize with users' problems in different ways and sketched ideas and defined MVP and ended up the Lo-fi wireframes, tested it with customers. And then I identified new problems and sketched again.
