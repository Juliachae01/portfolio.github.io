About me


My name is Julia Chae, I am currently based in Stockholm and studying UX-Design.
I have a background in fine art, digital media design and curatorial studies, and worked in various roles in the art museum field for several years in both Seoul and Stockholm.

At that time I have curated public programs for museum visitors, which made me focus on understanding visitors' behaviour and expectations. These drove me to have a great interest in customer experience, behavioural science and usability.

Moreover, as a person, I am also passionate about meeting people, listening to people's needs and voices and designing user-centred solutions, especially in the digital sphere.
That's why I want to dig into the UX today!
